Lasagna

Description

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling. A more traditional homemade lasagna filling would be made with ricotta but my mom's recipe calls for a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan. I have found none better anywhere. Serve with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Bertoli Spaghetti Sauce
  • Butter
  • Ground Beef
  • No-Boil Lasagna Noodles

Steps to make out lasagna

  1. The first step is to preheat your oven to 350 F. Next, brown your ground beef. You can always substitute ground beef with ground turkey, chicken, or sausage. You can really chose whatever you and your family prefer most. Once browned, drain the grease.
  2. Next, lay out all of your ingredients so you can begin to layer the lasagna. First, spread about ¼ Cup of pasta sauce along the base of the baking dish.
  3. Next, spread 1/3 of your beef mixture on top of the sauce, evenly distributing.
  4. Once meat is on, layer the top of the meat with 1/4th of the cheddar cheese.
  5. Then, place the noodle sheets on top. You can cut the edges or break a few in half if necessary to make it fit your particular dish.
  6. Last, place dabs of the butter on top of the noodles. Do this combination of layering two more times.
  7. Once you have done all the layering, end by placing another 1/4th cup of sauce on the noodles and sprinkling with cheese.
  8. Bake for 40 minutes. Enjoy!
